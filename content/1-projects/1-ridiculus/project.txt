Title: Working Together Publication

----

Text: 

Kingscott had gone through a many changes, with  dramatic shift in leadership structure, new hires, and the acquisition of a new company. The goal was to communicate all of these changes to clients, shareholders, and community members while celebrating our latest clients and their successes.  

Content was written and curated in collaboration with the Marketing Coordinator and Marketing Assistant, with project updates to the Leadership team at 20%, 50%, and 99%. Final product was 24 pages, 9"x10.75" was sent to River Run Press and printed on Sappi Flo.
